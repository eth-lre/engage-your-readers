Thank you for participating in our study! We are developing a tool to enhance textbooks with AI and aim to understand your experience when engaging with textbooks. This task has 3 parts with instructions on the top of the page.
  1. Demographic questionnaire
  2. Read an article
  3. Answer article-related questions
This project is a collaboration at ETH Zürich, by Xiaoyu Zhang, Peng Cui, Vilém Zouhar, Mrinmaya Sachan, and others. The goal of this research is to develop techniques to improve human reading comprehension. The estimated completion time is 30-60 minutes.
