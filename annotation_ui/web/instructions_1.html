Reading Comprehension - Guidelines
This task has 3 subsequent parts.

1. Article reading

  • Read an article very carefully.
  • You have 20 minutes for this part. Manage your time to ensure that you can read the entire article at a comfortable pace.
  • As you read, we will display a series of questions on the right side, each linked to its context within the article. These questions are designed to help you understand the content. When you encounter a question, make sure to read and comprehend it before moving on.
  • You are not required to answer these questions immediately. Instead, think about the questions and keep them in mind as you proceed with reading.

2. Article-related questions

  • After reading, we ask you to answer a few questions about the article. Your answers should be exclusively based on the article's content, without incorporating personal opinions or external information.
  • The use of any external tool, such as search engines, is not allowed in this part. Your true response is crucial for the validity of our research.
  • You can not go back to the article during this period.

3. Project-related questions

  • At the end you will be asked questions about the interface and the experiment.
  • This is the most important part of your participation, so please pay particular attention to your answers.
